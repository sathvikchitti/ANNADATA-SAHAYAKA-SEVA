Guide for Cotton Cultivation

Fertilizer Schedule

  • Basal Dose: Apply 25kg Urea, 50kg DAP, and 25kg MOP per acre at sowing.
  • First Top Dressing (30-35 days): Apply 25kg Urea per acre.
  • Second Top Dressing (55-60 days): Apply 25kg Urea per acre.

Pest Management

  • Pink Bollworm: Use Pheromone traps (5/acre). Spray Thiodicarb 75 WP @ 1gm/litre of water.
  • Aphids/Jassids: Spray Imidacloprid 17.8 SL @ 0.3 ml/litre of water.
  • Safety: Always wear protective gloves and mask during application. Spray during evening hours.
